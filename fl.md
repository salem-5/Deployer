# Factory Logistics Compatibility

But wait... why?

We truly did our best to bring compatibility with Factory Logistics (FL). However, since their GitHub repository has been archived, we decided to move forward with our own fluid packaging system.

We believe our alternative, [Repackaged](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/repackaged), is highly stable and makes the most of the Deployer API. We highly recommend giving it a try!

That said, we are still open to community contributions. We will gladly accept any pull requests that provide valid compatibility fixes for Create: Factory Logistics, as we’d love to see both mods working together seamlessly one day.
